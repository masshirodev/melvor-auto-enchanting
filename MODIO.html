Bulk tools for the Enchanting mod. Auto Enchanting adds bank-wide disenchanting, enchant-to-grade jobs, reroll-until-modifier jobs, and one unified auto-disenchant panel for new loot.

Requires the Enchanting mod.

What it does

  • Auto-disenchant new loot - takes over the Enchanting mod's own drop/reward auto-disenchant options so every control lives in one panel.
  • Disenchant the bank - disenchant all unlocked enchanted bank stacks at or below a grade you choose.
  • Enchant until - push one selected item, or every eligible unlocked bank item, up to a target grade.
  • Reroll until - reroll one enchanted item until the modifiers you picked appear, with a hard reroll cap.

Safety defaults

Automation is off by default. Every job that spends items or essence is started by hand from the Enchanting page, and locked items are always skipped.

  • Skill bank disenchant uses the real Disenchant action and gives full XP.
  • Instant bank disenchant does not use the action slot, but gives the same half XP as the Enchanting mod's native auto-disenchant.
  • Enchanting stops before any essence would fall below your configured floor.
  • Rerolling stops when the selected modifiers are present or the cap is reached.

Usage

Enable it alongside the Enchanting mod, load a character, and open the Enchanting page. The Auto Enchanting panel sits under the XP bars. When the master switch is on, the original Enchanting auto-disenchant row is hidden and restored when you turn Auto Enchanting off.

Good to know

The mod automates clicks and item operations while the game is open. It does not simulate missed work while offline.
